# Aether-Link: User Guide (How to Use)

Welcome to Aether-Link! This guide explains how to control your computer using only your hand gestures and voice. No mouse or keyboard required.

---

## 🔒 Getting Started: Unlocking
When you start the app, it is **LOCKED** for security. You must use your unique "Air-Signature" to enter.

| Step | Action | What Happens |
|------|--------|--------------|
| **1. Start** | Push your hand forward quickly (Air-Push) | Recording starts (you have 3 seconds) |
| **2. Draw** | Draw your signature pattern in the air | The camera tracks your hand |
| **3. Result** | Wait a moment | If correct, the system unlocks to the **HOME** menu |

*Note: If you fail 3 times, look at the terminal window for instructions on how to reset.*

---

## 🏠 HOME Menu: Choosing a Mode
Once unlocked, you are in the Home Menu. You can pick where to go by pointing at a button.

| To go here... | Point your hand at... | And then... |
|---------------|----------------------|-------------|
| **Media** | "Media" button | **Air-Push** or **Hold Still (Dwell)** for 1.5s |
| **Mouse** | "Mouse" button | **Air-Push** or **Hold Still (Dwell)** for 1.5s |
| **Tabs** | "Tab" button | **Air-Push** or **Hold Still (Dwell)** for 1.5s |
| **Windows** | "Window" button | **Air-Push** or **Hold Still (Dwell)** for 1.5s |
| **Keyboard** | "Keyboard" button | Show the typing overlay |
| **Exit** | "Exit" button | Close the app |

*   **Pinch Gesture:** Touch your thumb and index finger together to show/hide the keyboard at any time.
*   **Peace Sign:** Hold up 2 fingers (Peace sign) to take a screenshot.

---

## 🎵 MEDIA Mode (Music & Video)
Control your movies or music with simple swipes.

| Action | Gesture |
|--------|---------|
| **Play / Pause** | Open your palm (all 5 fingers out) |
| **Volume Up** | Swipe your hand UP quickly |
| **Volume Down** | Swipe your hand DOWN quickly |
| **Next Song** | Swipe your hand RIGHT |
| **Previous Song** | Swipe your hand LEFT |

---

## 🖱️ MOUSE Mode (Desktop Control)
Control your computer cursor with your hand.

| Action | How to do it |
|--------|--------------|
| **Move Cursor** | Just move your hand. It's faster when you lean back! |
| **Left Click** | Push your hand forward quickly (**Air-Push**) |
| **Go Back** | Move cursor to the top-center "BACK" button and hold still |

---

## 🌐 TAB Mode (Browser Control)
Easily switch between website tabs.

| Action | Gesture |
|--------|---------|
| **Next Tab** | Swipe your hand RIGHT |
| **Previous Tab** | Swipe your hand LEFT |
| **Close Tab** | Push your hand forward (**Air-Push**) on the "Close" button |
| **New Tab** | Push your hand forward (**Air-Push**) on the "New" button |

---

## 🪟 WINDOW Mode (App Switcher)
Switch between open apps like Chrome, Spotify, or Folders.

| Action | Gesture |
|--------|---------|
| **Next App** | Swipe your hand RIGHT |
| **Previous App** | Swipe your hand LEFT |
| **Task View** | Swipe your hand UP |
| **Minimize App** | Swipe your hand DOWN |

---

## 🎙️ Voice Commands (Speak clearly)
You can say these commands at any time (except when locked). **Speak the exact phrase.**

| Category | What to Say |
|----------|-------------|
| **Navigation** | `"go home"`, `"mouse mode"`, `"media mode"`, `"go back"` |
| **Clicking** | `"click"`, `"double click"`, `"right click"` |
| **Typing** | `"type [your message]"` (Example: *"type hello everyone"*) |
| **Media** | `"play music"`, `"pause music"`, `"volume up"`, `"volume down"` |
| **Tabs** | `"next tab"`, `"previous tab"`, `"close tab"` |
| **Windows** | `"next window"`, `"minimize window"`, `"task view"` |
| **System** | `"take screenshot"`, `"show keyboard"`, `"lock system"`, `"exit app"` |

---

## 💡 Pro Tips for Best Experience
1.  **Distance Matters:** The system is smartest when you are between 50cm and 1.5 meters away.
2.  **Stay Still to Click:** If Air-Push feels hard, just **Hold your hand still** over a button for 1.5 seconds. It will click automatically!
3.  **Voice Accuracy:** Wait for a split second after the app starts before giving your first voice command.
4.  **Hands Busy?** You can control almost everything using just your voice if your hands are resting.

The Problem — Why This Was Needed

  • Hygiene & Sterility: In hospitals and labs, touching a shared mouse or keyboard spreads infection — surgeons cannot touch anything non-sterile.
  • Accessibility: Users with limited hand mobility struggle to use a physical mouse or type on small keys.
  • "Gorilla Arm" Fatigue: Older gesture systems required large arm movements — users got exhausted within minutes.
  • Inaccuracy of Old Systems: Background noise triggered fake clicks. The cursor would jump or jitter at the moment of clicking.
🏥

during an operation.

Our Solution — Touchless 3D Interface

3D Gesture Control

An Orbbec depth camera tracks your hand in three dimensions (X, Y, Z) at 30 frames per second. Cursor moves exactly where you point.

Offline Voice Commands

Vosk speech engine listens for 37 exact phrases — completely offline. No internet required. No data sent to Google or any cloud service.

3D Biometric Lock

The system starts locked. Only drawing your unique 3D air-signature unlocks it. A flat photo or video cannot fool the system.

Core Design Goal: Replace the mouse and keyboard entirely — no touch, no screen, no special room — just your hand and your voice.

3 / 12

The Core Design Rule — Why Both?

Camera — Spatial Actions
  • Cursor Movement: Real-time 30fps X/Y/Z tracking
  • Scrolling: Swipe velocity = scroll speed naturally
  • 3D Signature: Only depth camera can capture 3D trajectory
  • Dwell-Click: Hover 1.5s = auto-click, zero extra effort
Voice — Named Actions
  • Text Input: "type hello" is 30x faster than air-keyboard
  • Click: Air-push shakes cursor; voice keeps it perfectly still
  • Mode Switch: "go to mouse" is instant vs multiple swipes
  • Hands Free: Works even when hands are resting or dirty

The Rule: Camera = continuous spatial data  |  Voice = discrete named commands. They never overlap.

System Architecture — 3 Threads

Thread 1

Camera Pipeline

  • Capture color + depth frames
  • D2C alignment fuses streams
  • MediaPipe detects 21 landmarks
  • 1€ Filter smooths X, Y, Z
  • Hand identity reject teleports
Thread 2

Voice Engine

  • PyAudio streams 16kHz mono
  • Vosk KaldiRecognizer parses
  • Exact-match against 37 commands
  • 0.5s cooldown rate-limiter
  • Puts result in thread-safe queue
Main Thread

UI Loop (33ms)

  • Reads latest frame via Lock
  • Blocked during LOCKED state
  • Routes gesture + voice commands
  • Updates PyQt6 HUD overlay
  • Sends actions to OS via pyautogui

Thread Safety:  threading.Lock protects frame data  |  queue.Queue passes voice commands safely

Security — 3D Biometric Air-Signature

1

Air-Push to Start

Push your finger forward 5cm quickly. Recording begins.

2

Draw in 3D Space

Move your hand in your unique pattern for 3 seconds including Z-depth movement.

3

DTW Verification

FastDTW compares shape against reference. Works even if drawn faster or slower.

✓

Unlocked!

System transitions to HOME menu. All modes become available.

Anti-Spoofing: Z-Variance Check

The system requires a minimum Z-variance of 100 mm². A flat 2D gesture, a photo, or a video replay is automatically rejected because it has no real depth variation.

Voice Blocked While Locked

All 37 voice commands are silently drained when the system is locked. No one can say "unlock system" to bypass security — only the physical 3D signature works.

Advanced Algorithms — Smart, Fast & Stable

Z-Gain Cursor Scaling

Cursor speed adapts to your distance from the camera.

DistanceSpeed
30 cm (close)0.7× — Slow, precise
50 cm (desk)1.0× — Normal
150 cm (far)2.0× — Fast, wide

Formula: gain = (z / 500mm)^0.6

1 Euro Filter (Smoothing)

A speed-adaptive mathematical filter — the smartest cursor smoother available.

✋ Hand is STILL

Maximum smoothing applied → zero jitter on screen

✋ Hand MOVING FAST

Smoothing removed instantly → zero lag, follows perfectly

Hand Identity Tracking

Prevents the cursor from "teleporting" if a second person enters the camera view.

Physics Fact

A real wrist cannot move more than 200 pixels in 33ms — that requires 6 m/s.

If wrist jump > 200px/frame → rejected as a different hand. Previous safe position is kept.

Control Modes & Voice Commands

5 Operational Modes (Camera)

ModeKey Gestures
🔒 LockedAir-Push → draw 3D signature
🏠 HomePoint + Air-Push or Dwell 1.5s on buttons
🖱️ MouseMove hand → cursor; Air-Push → click
🎵 MediaOpen Palm = Play; Swipe Up/Down = Volume
🪟 Window/TabSwipe Right/Left to switch apps or tabs

Voice Commands (37 total)

  • "go to mouse" — Switch to Mouse Mode
  • "click" — Left-click at cursor position
  • "type hello world" — Types anywhere on screen
  • "play music" — Toggle play/pause
  • "minimize window" — Win+Down
  • "take screenshot" — Win+PrintScreen
  • "lock system" — Re-lock, require signature

Safety Rule: Exact multi-word match only. No fuzzy matching — prevents false triggers from background speech.

100% Offline — Privacy & Performance

Zero Internet Dependency

No audio, video, or hand data is ever sent to any server. Vosk STT and MediaPipe both run locally on your CPU.

No GPU Required

MediaPipe uses TensorFlow Lite — optimized for CPU. Works on any standard laptop or desktop without an expensive graphics card.

Low Latency

Voice recognition: ~200ms. Gesture response: ~33ms (30fps camera). No network round-trip delays.
